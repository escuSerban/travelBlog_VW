Cramond Beach Trip

distance traveled: 400 miles

Camping by the North Sea.

Anyone who really loves camping knows to expect problems and discomfort on every camping trip. Part of the joy of camping comes in the suffering.

My wife and I decided to camp for one night nearby the sea coast. We managed to find a slightly remote area just at the end of the promenade of Cramond Beach.

Everything was great apart from the fast blowing wind. Even if you are camping on a van you it still has an impact on your night.

Enjoying Edinburgh's attractions.

Next day we went to Edinburgh city centre, where we had some breakfast. After that we visited Edinburgh Castle, which has a rich history and impressed us a lot.

Then, we continued to enjoy the local cuisine at "Stac Polly ", a ground-floor brasserie in stone cellars.

It was indeed an extraordinary experience and we will definetly come back again sometime.
